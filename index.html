The 5 Rules

Carry these all day

1. Personal par is +2. Every bogey is a birdie in your book. Score vs 108, not 72.

2. Aim left, drift right. Left edge of every fairway, left-centre of every green — except where water is right.

3. The water rule. Never attempt a carry over water longer than a comfy 7-iron (130). Lay up to a full PW or SW number instead. 14 lakes out there — this rule is worth 6–10 shots.

4. Middle of the green, always. Pins don't exist today. Lag every first putt to a 3-ft circle.

5. 7-iron owns 130. The 6i stays in the bag all day. 5Hy only for long shots off tight or scruffy lies.

Heat Protocol 45°C — not optional

Every hole

Drink before you're thirsty: a bottle every 2–3 holes, alternating water and electrolytes. Start before the 1st tee.

Cold towel on the neck between shots. Park the buggy in shade whenever it exists.

Salty snack mid-front and mid-back nine. Sunscreen again at the turn.

Your back: after any long buggy stretch, 2–3 slow hip-hinge rehearsals before you swing. Never go seated → full swing.

Stop signals

Cramping · dizziness · nausea · pounding headache · chills or goosebumps in the heat · you notice you've stopped sweating.

Any one of these: round over. Shade, cold towels, drink, clubhouse. No negotiation — you're playing solo, so you are your own marshal.
